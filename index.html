Remi Taggering V2 – Privacy Policy

Date: 7/7/25

Welcome to Remi Taggering V2, a multiplayer game developed using PlayFab and Photon technologies. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and disclose your information and what rights you have in relation to your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account and Identity Information (via PlayFab):

  • UserID (automatically generated)
  • Display name or username
  • Email address (if provided for registration or login)
  • IP address
  • Device ID
  • Country or region
  • Login history
  • Linked third-party accounts (e.g., Steam, Google)

1.2 Gameplay and Technical Data:

  • Game session data (start/end times, session duration)
  • Player stats, achievements, progress, and leaderboard rankings
  • Matchmaking and connection logs (via Photon)
  • Error reports and crash logs
  • In-game purchases and virtual currency balances

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Account creation and management through PlayFab
  • Multiplayer matchmaking and connectivity using Photon services
  • Saving and loading game progress, player stats, and preferences
  • Providing customer support and resolving bugs or crashes
  • Analyzing gameplay data to improve game experience
  • Enforcing game rules, terms of use, and anti-cheat policies
  • Optional email communications for updates, promotions, or feedback (if opted in)

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

3.1 PlayFab (by Microsoft):

Used for account management, data storage, analytics, and user authentication. PlayFab complies with Microsoft’s security and privacy practices.

3.2 Photon (by Exit Games):

Used for real-time multiplayer services and networking. Photon stores minimal connection and session data necessary for performance and anti-fraud measures.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties.

4. Data Protection Measures

  • Encryption: All communication between your device and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS.
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel can access user data for support or maintenance.
  • Data Minimization: We collect only what is necessary to operate and improve the game.
  • Regular Security Audits: Our systems and third-party platforms undergo regular security reviews.

5. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we have stored about you.
  • Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Delete your user account and all associated data.

6. Children's Privacy

Remi Taggering V2 is not intended for users under the age of 13 (or equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we learn we have inadvertently collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.
